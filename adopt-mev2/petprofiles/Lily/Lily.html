MEET LILY!

Age: 1.5 years

Breed: Ragdoll

Size: Medium

Sex: Female

Coat Maintenance: Moderate

Activity Level: Moderate

CHARACTERISTICS:

Meet Lily, the graceful Ragdoll cat with a serene presence and beautiful blue eyes. Lily is a calm and affectionate feline companion, ready to bring tranquility to any home.

Lily's soft, semi-longhair coat showcases the classic color patterns of a Ragdoll cat. Her gentle disposition and laid-back nature make her an excellent choice for those seeking a relaxed and loving cat. Lily enjoys being pampered with gentle strokes and often follows her human companions around the house.

This sweet Ragdoll is known for her easygoing nature and compatibility with families. Lily is content to spend lazy afternoons lounging in comfortable spots and is always ready for a cozy cuddle session. She is an ideal choice for those looking for a loving and low-maintenance feline friend.

Lily is eagerly waiting for a forever home where she can bring joy and calmness. Adopt Lily and experience the soothing presence of this beautiful Ragdoll cat!
