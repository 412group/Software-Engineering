CHARACTERISTICS:

Meet Fluffy, a charming 5-year-old orange tabby with a unique personality. Despite his grumpy exterior, Fluffy is a playful and affectionate feline friend who enjoys the simple pleasures of life.

Fluffy loves interactive play and may occasionally display his independent nature. While he may not be fond of sharing his space with other pets, he could be the perfect companion for someone looking for a one-pet household.

His playfulness and occasional grumpiness add character to his charm. Fluffy appreciates a cozy spot for napping and a good scratch behind the ears when he's in the mood for some affection. But dont scratch him for too long! He might scratch you back.

This mature orange tabby is looking for a quiet and understanding forever home where he can spend his days lounging in the sun and enjoying the company of a patient owner.

Fluffy is ready to cause some serious wreckage to your home!
