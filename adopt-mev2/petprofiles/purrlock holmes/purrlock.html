CHARACTERISTICS:

Meet Purrlock Holmes, the master of feline deduction and hilarity! This 3-year-old Siamese sensation is not your typical detective; he's on a mission to solve the mystery of making everyone laugh.

Purrlock is a goofy and funny cat who embraces the art of play with unmatched enthusiasm. His comical antics and silly behavior will turn any mundane moment into a burst of laughter.

Don't be surprised if Purrlock Holmes decides to embark on his own midnight mystery tour with the infamous "zoomies" at 3 am. His playful energy knows no bounds, and he's always up for a game of chase or a hilarious high-speed run around the house.

While Purrlock is the undisputed king of comedy, he also has a sweet side. When he's not busy being the class clown, he enjoys curling up in cozy spots for a quick catnap and welcomes affectionate scratches behind the ears.

This Siamese sensation is looking for a forever home where laughter is the main language, and the joy of having a goofy feline companion is fully appreciated. Purrlock Holmes is on the case to steal your heart!
